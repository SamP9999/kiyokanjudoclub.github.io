What to Expect in Your First Class

  • No experience needed

    We welcome complete beginners and teach fundamentals from day one.

  • What to wear

    Comfortable athletic clothing (t-shirt and sweatpants). Gi uniforms available for purchase.

  • Arrive 10 minutes early

    Time to meet your instructor, tour the facility, and get oriented.

  • Respectful environment

    We emphasize discipline, respect, and mutual support in all classes.

  • Have fun!

    Judo is challenging but rewarding. Enjoy the journey and make new friends.
